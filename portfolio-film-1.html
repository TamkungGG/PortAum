Short Film: 我爱上了尖竹汶府

  • Category: Short Film
  • Director: Tanchanok Khangkasri
  • Date: 2023
  • Film URL: Available on Bilibili

This film proposal presents a story of a TikTok creator who comes to Thailand in search of a popular fruit but ends up discovering the beauty of a new place and forming a bond with a local resident. The film showcases the beauty of Chanthaburi and the hospitality of its people, and highlights the importance of stepping outside of one's comfort zones and embracing new experiences. The film's themes of discovery, culture, and friendship will resonate with audiences and make for an enjoyable and memorable viewing experience.

As the director and actor of "我爱上了尖竹汶府" I brought the story to life with their passion for filmmaking and acting. I was responsible for overseeing the entire production process, from pre-production planning to post-production editing. I also played the lead role of Mei, delivering a powerful and emotional performance that brought the story to life.

The making of "我爱上了尖竹汶府" was a challenging but rewarding experience for me. I was able to showcase their skills as a director and actor, bringing their vision for the film to life and delivering a powerful and impactful story to the audience.

I'm is proud to have participated in the short film contest in China and is excited to see where this journey takes them in their career as a filmmaker and actor.
